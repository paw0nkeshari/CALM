📘 What is Bookkeeping & Accounting?

Bookkeeping records your daily transactions. Accounting interprets this data to help you make financial decisions, manage compliance, and grow your business.

📊 Why Do You Need It?

  • ✅ Understand your profit/loss clearly
  • ✅ Ensure GST & Tax compliance
  • ✅ Make smarter financial decisions
  • ✅ Smooth loan & audit processing
  • ✅ Detect financial leaks early
